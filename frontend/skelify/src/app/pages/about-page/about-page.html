What is Skelify?

Skelify is a smart skeleton generator designed to accelerate the software development lifecycle. By automating the repetitive and time-consuming tasks of project setup and configuration, Skelify allows developers to focus on what matters most: building great software.

Our Mission

Our mission is to provide a powerful and flexible tool that enforces best practices, ensures consistency across projects, and dramatically reduces the time it takes to get from idea to a working skeleton.

Our Team

Skelify is built by a passionate team of developers at ELCA.
